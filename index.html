Reviews

Janis Smith

Fantastic food! Chicken empanada appetizer was delicious! Husband loved his ceviche. The pork and shrimp entrees were excellent with yellow rice and flavorful black beans. The coconut custard is unbelievable! Sorry we didn’t find this gem sooner so we could go back and try more!

Robert Jennings

A hidden gem with great food and ambiance. Quality was outstanding. Flavors were truly memorable. The night we were there they had a guitarist playing a classical guitar. The volume was loud enough to enjoy, but not so loud that it was difficult to hold a conversation. Definitely a place to return to.

Tara Rose

I go to this restaurant every time I am in the area and the quality is always great. The food is always full of flavor and presentations are excellent. I’ve brought many different people to Orange Plate and have tried the roasted pork, lamb chops, seafood platter, and many others that taste great. Portion sizes will leave you satisfied or even enough for a snack the next day.

Sarah Wiggins

Small but very friendly place. Service was spot on and the food was very good. Didn't know what to expect going in - but was pleasantly suprised and will go back when I am in town.
